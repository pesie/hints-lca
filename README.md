# hints-lca

latent class analysis of health information experience using HINTS 7 (2024) data.

identifies distinct profiles of perceived information inconsistency and overload among U.S. adults, and examines how these profiles relate to trust in health institutions and perceived quality of care.

## data

Health Information National Trends Survey (HINTS 7), 2024. nationally representative sample of U.S. adults. data available from the NCI:
[https://hints.cancer.gov/data/download-data.aspx](https://hints.cancer.gov/data/download-data.aspx#H7)

## analysis

**segmentation variables**
- health recommendations conflict with each other
- health recommendations keep changing
- too many recommendations to keep track of
- everything seems to cause cancer

**outcomes**
- trust in healthcare system, doctors, scientists, and government
- perceived quality of care

## dependencies

tidyverse, poLCA, survey, psych
